Secret Key


Your Secret Key is the key to your account and personal information. It is crucial that you keep it private and secure. Do not share it with anyone, as anyone with access to your Secret Key can control your account and access your information. Store it in a safe place, such as a password manager or a secure offline location. Remember, if you lose your Secret Key, you will lose access to your account permanently.
